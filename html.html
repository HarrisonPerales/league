League of legends is five versus five team game with the main objective of destroying your opponent's base.

There are many characters or champions to choose from, each having unique abilites and ways of fighting your opponent. With over 160 champions to choose from its smart to look at what they can do before hand. Click here to learn more about all the champions

After choosing your champion you want to choose your lane, this is where you will go on the map to fight until later on in the game.

Minions will spawn and walk down each lane and start fighting. You want to kill your opponents minions while protecting your own. Killing minions or your opponent gives you gold which can be used to buy items to become stronger.

Overtime you will gain experience from killing minions and opponents which gives your champion levels, each level you gain can be spent to upgrade abilities on your champion.

Eventually with enough items and levels you can push to your opponents main building, the core, and destroy it winning the game.
